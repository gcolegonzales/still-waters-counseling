# Still Waters Counseling &amp; Therapy — website concept

A premium, calm, spa-like single-page website for **Still Waters Counseling &amp; Therapy LLC** —
a faith-based individual &amp; couples counseling practice in Louisiana, offered **online only** by a
Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW).

This is an **unsolicited redesign concept** created as a "this could be yours" pitch piece.

## What's here

- Serene, dusty-slate-blue &amp; mist-white aesthetic with a warm-cream accent, matching the practice's brand card.
- A recurring, subtle **water-drop / ripple motif** (animated in the hero and the scripture moment).
- Elegant type: a script wordmark for "Still Waters" over a letter-spaced serif sub-line.
- Editorial, varied section layouts (no repeated icon-card grid): hero, welcome, about/approach,
  values row, featured specialties + grouped expertise, therapy modalities (dark band), who I help,
  credentials, fees table, a full-bleed Psalm 23:2 moment, and a schedule/contact block.
- The **money feature**: always-easy booking — click-to-call in the header, hero, and contact;
  a real "Request an Appointment" inquiry form (opens the visitor's email client — no fake calendar);
  plus direct email and hours.
- Polished, performant motion: shrink-on-scroll header, animated nav underline, real motion mobile
  menu, `IntersectionObserver` scroll reveals, subtle ripple animation. Fully respects
  `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- Accessible &amp; semantic HTML, responsive from 360px phones to widescreen, and a caring footer with
  the **988** Suicide &amp; Crisis Lifeline note plus a not-medical-advice disclaimer.

## How to view

Open `index.html` in any browser (double-click it), or serve the folder statically. No build step,
no framework — just `index.html`, `styles.css`, `script.js`, and `assets/`.

## Assets / imagery

- The practice's real brand card was optimized (2.3&nbsp;MB PNG → ~165&nbsp;KB JPG) and a clean,
  text-free strip of the misty-water photo was cropped for the hero. See `assets/`.
- `assets/photos/` is available for any additional real photos the practice wants to add later.
